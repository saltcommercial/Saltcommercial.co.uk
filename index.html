About

Most hospitality businesses have the product right. The food is good, the space works, the team cares. What is usually missing is the commercial structure to match. Revenue gets left to chance. Marketing runs without a plan. Opportunities go unanswered because nobody owns them.

salt. is the senior commercial function your business needs but does not have. Not a consultant who delivers a report and moves on. A commercial partner who builds the systems, works alongside the team and stays accountable for what happens next. Whether you run a single restaurant, a growing pub group, a venue or an event catering business, the commercial discipline is the same. salt. makes sure it gets the attention it deserves.
